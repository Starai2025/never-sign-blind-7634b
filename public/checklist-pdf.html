"Before you sign, ask these 7 questions. If you answer "No" or "Not Sure" to any of them — do not sign blind."

1
Is there a clear outside deadline for payment?
If not, your payment may drift longer than expected — with no legal deadline to force it.
2
Is retainage release tied to your completed scope — not overall project closeout?
If not, your money may stay stuck 60–90+ days after your last day on site.
3
Can the GC backcharge you only with prior notice and documentation?
If not, deductions may hit your payment with little warning and no recourse.
4
Are notice deadlines for extra work realistic in real field conditions?
A 48-hour written notice window is almost impossible to hit consistently on a live job.
5
Are liquidated damages capped and clearly tied to your actual scope?
Without a cap tied to your scope, delay risk may be broader than the job is worth.
6
Is your responsibility limited to what your company actually controls?
Broad indemnity and flow-down clauses can push GC or owner risk directly onto you.
7
Are final payment and retainage release conditions clearly defined?
Vague closeout language can hold money you've already earned behind conditions you can't control.
