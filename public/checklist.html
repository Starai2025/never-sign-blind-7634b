SUBCONTRACT CASH TRAP CHECKLIST™
Never Sign Blind™ — Profit Protection for Subcontractors

"Before you sign, ask these 7 questions. If you answer 'No' or 'Not sure' to any of them, do not sign blind."

Is there a clear outside deadline for payment?
If not, your payment may drift longer than expected.
Is retainage tied to your scope — not full project closeout?
If not, your money may stay stuck long after your work is done.
Can the GC backcharge only with notice and documentation?
If not, deductions may hit later with little warning.
Are notice deadlines for extra work realistic?
Are liquidated damages capped and tied to your scope?
Is your responsibility limited to what you control?
Are final payment and retainage release clearly defined?
